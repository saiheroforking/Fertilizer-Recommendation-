Fertilizer Recommendation System

In modern agriculture, optimal crop production heavily relies on balanced fertilization. Fertilizers play a crucial role in supplementing essential nutrients that are often deficient in the soil. However, misuse or overuse can reduce crop quality and pollute the environment. Our system smartly analyzes plant diseases and suggests the best fertilizers for sustainable farming.

Fertilizer

Fertilizers are substances that are added to soil or plants to provide essential nutrients that are necessary for healthy growth. These nutrients help plants to grow, develop, and produce better yields, especially when the soil is deficient in them. Fertilizers can be organic (e.g., compost, manure) or synthetic (chemical fertilizers).
